Reporting_Infringement

To initialize a takedown request, the notification must include:

  • /01 Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner.
  • /02 Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • /03 Direct transmission URIs (MixMint asset IDs/links) of the infringing node.
  • /04 Contact coordinates: Email address and physical address.
