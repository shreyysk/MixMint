Security & Trust

How we protect DJs and Buyers on India's premier music marketplace.

🛡️ Buyer Protection

Ownership, Not Access: When you buy on MixMint, you are purchasing a perpetual license. No subscriptions, no DRM, and no "revoked access" due to licensing changes.

Download Insurance: Our optional ₹15 insurance guarantees that even if a DJ removes a track from the store, you can still download your purchase from our encrypted archive.

Byte-Level Verification: Every download is verified via SHA-256 checksums to ensure the file you receive is exactly what the DJ uploaded, with no corruption or tampering.

🎧 DJ Security

Anti-Piracy (IP Lock): To prevent link sharing, every download link is locked to the buyer's IP address and device fingerprint for 48 hours.

Escrow Policy: All earnings are held in a secure escrow account (24-48 hours) to protect against chargebacks and fraudulent behavior, ensuring a clean marketplace.

Secure R2 Storage: Your tracks are stored in private, cryptographically secured buckets (Cloudflare R2). No public URLs ever exist for your high-quality files.

🔒 Technical Integrity

Session Isolation

We use strict IP-bound session management. If your IP changes significantly mid-session, we auto-terminate the session to prevent hijacking.

Audit Logging

Every administrative action and financial transaction is logged in our immutable audit trail for complete transparency.
