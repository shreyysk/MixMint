⚠️ Legal Liability & DJ Responsibility

MixMint operates strictly as a secure distribution infrastructure, providing encrypted sync capabilities between independent Creator Nodes (DJs) and their audience. All digital assets hosted on the platform are uploaded, controlled, and directly verified by the respective uploader.

We enforce a zero-tolerance policy for DMCA violations or unauthorized distribution of intellectual property. Upon receiving a valid DMCA request, MixMint acts as an automated mediator to terminate the offending node, permanently archive the infringement payload, and forfeit all pending yields to the copyright holder.

By initializing a download or utilizing this infrastructure, all liability regarding content legality remains exclusively with the deploying DJ. MixMint is held harmless from copyright disputes under DMCA Safe Harbor protocols.
