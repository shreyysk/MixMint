MixMint Security Standards

MixMint is built on a "Controlled Ownership" architecture. Unlike streaming platforms, we treat music as a secure digital asset. Here is how we protect our DJs and Listeners.

1. Secure Token System

Every download on MixMint is powered by our proprietary Secure Proxy Engine. We never expose direct file links to the public or the browser.

  • One-Time Use: Tokens expire immediately after a successful download.
  • IP Binding: Tokens are cryptographically bound to your IP address.
  • Device Fingerprinting: Downloads are locked to the specific device used for purchase.

Piracy Resistance

Metadata watermarks are injected into every file, linking the asset to our internal DJ ledger for anti-resale enforcement.

Byte Verification

Our proxy verifies 100% byte completion and SHA-256 checksums before marking a purchase as successful.

2. Fraud Protection

We use active pattern detection to identify and block scraping attempts. Suspicious behavior triggers automatic speed throttling and session invalidation.

3. Transparency & Rights

Deleted content is archived immutably for legal compliance (DMCA/Copyright). DJs retain 100% ownership of their rights, while buyers get permanent personal-use licenses verified by our ledger.
